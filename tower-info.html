Tower Rangers

Tower Rangers is a tower defense game built within Unreal Engine 5. Unlike other tower defense games, it distinguishes itself by offering players direct control over their avatar within a dynamic 3D setting. The game contains multiple levels with different maps, each increasing in difficulty. Like other tower defense games, there is an in-game currency system to purchase different towers with the objective of eliminating enemy NPCs before they reach the player's base.

Skills and methods:

  • Unreal Engine 5: Provided a robust framework for developing and designing in a 3D game environment, as well as facilitating the integration of high-resolution textures to deliver top-tier graphics.
  • C++: The language used to write the blueprint of the game.
  • Planning: Effective planning was paramount for advancing in our five-member project. We employed various strategies, such as maintaining a product and sprint backlog, along with utilizing a burndown chart, to ensure deadlines were met consistently.
